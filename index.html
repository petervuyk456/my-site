Hey there stranger, thanks for stopping by!

I'm finished with the cheesy intro, all jokes aside.

To learn a bit about me, you'll need to close your eyes.
Hours in that thinking state is how I spend a lot of time.

I yearn to grow and better, no different than all of you.
I enjoy country music and pullups... okay, perhaps stranger than some of you.

In high school I was a wrestler, it taught me to persevere.
As an undergrad I did research, and learned to mechanically engineer.

I love complex math and puzzles - by complex, I mean new to me!
Perhaps that inspired me to code, and all its intricacy.

I started learning Python, and grew fond of it indeed!
But soon I learned that scripting had a lot more to it you see.

To my keyboard warriors out there, I know you know what I mean.
It takes more than swifty keystrokes, to get a repo from A to Z.

Somehow I never quit, despite package management and Git.
And now I'm seriously thankful, for every single add/commit.

It takes a lot to be a programmer, of that we can all agree.
Now throw data in the mix... I swear I'm just a little crazy.

You see failure is our only option: the catalyst to proficiency.
I've always loved the irony of failure yielding expertise.

Development and data are my newest hobbies (perhaps I got the wrong degree).
I thought about it a while... nah it's just adversity.

It may take me a little a longer, but one day I believe,
I'll be a full stack data scientist, or something in between.

Oh, and sometimes, I dabble with poetry.
